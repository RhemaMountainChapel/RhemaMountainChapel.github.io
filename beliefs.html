Fundamental Beliefs

The Bible

The Bible is inpired by God and it is "the infallible, authoritative rule of faith and conduct"

The Trinity of God

There is only one true God who exists as a Trinity. The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit. Jesus Christ is the son of God and as the second person of the Trinity, is God

Blessed Hope and the final judgement

The "imminent and blessed hope" of the church is its rupture preceeding the bodily return of Christ to earth. The rupture of the Church will be followed by the visible return of Christ and his reign on earth for a thousand years. There will also be a final judgement and eternal damnation for the "wicked dead"
